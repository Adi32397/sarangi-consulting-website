Investor-Ready Financial Documents

Sound financial advisory starts with the right documentation. These are the must-have documents that underpin every funding conversation and financial decision.

5
Cap Table

A live record of equity, options, and convertibles. Essential for calculating dilution, planning fundraises, and giving investors full transparency on ownership structure.

16
Pitch Deck

A compelling 10–15 slide deck that frames your financial story — market size, unit economics, growth levers, and the funding ask in investor-ready language.

17
Financial Model

A 3–5 year projection covering revenue forecasts, burn rate, unit economics, and funding milestones — the core deliverable of our financial advisory engagement.

18
Term Sheet

The foundational deal document outlining valuation, investment amount, board rights, and key investor protections. We help founders understand and negotiate every clause.
