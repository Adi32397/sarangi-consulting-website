About the Program

Sarthak is a targeted investment-readiness and seed funding program launched by Sarangi Consulting, to empower early-stage, revenue-generating startups. The program provides structured mentorship, financial planning support, and equity funding of up to ₹30 lakhs to help founders strengthen their business models, improve investor readiness, and scale sustainably.

Objective

1
Enable early-stage startups to become investment-ready through expert-led mentoring and financial planning.
2
Provide equity-based seed funding of up to ₹30 lakhs to help startups achieve key growth milestones.
3
Facilitate investor access and support scalable, revenue-generating startups on their path to institutional funding.

Key Component

The program is structured to provide holistic support across business fundamentals, capital injection, and industry connects.

Mentorship

1-1 mentoring sessions with top-tier sector experts, venture capitalists, and experienced Industry Experts to refine product-market fit and strategic operations.

Post-Funding Support

Ongoing guidance on systematic fund utilization, strict governance compliance, strategic milestones tracking, and coaching for follow-on fundraising rounds.

Networking Events

Direct exposure and pitching opportunities with potential angel investors, venture funds, corporate partners, commercial banks, and a network of fellow entrepreneurs.

Criteria

To maintain cohort quality, we select companies meeting these essential eligibility guidelines:

Registered and recognized by DPIIT.
Generating consistent revenue for at least 6 months.
Seeking to raise between ₹15 to ₹30 lakhs for scaling operations.

FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions about the Sarthak Seed Equity Support Scheme.

What is Sarthak?

Sarthak is an early-stage startup enablement, mentorship, and seed equity funding initiative launched by Sarangi Consulting to fast-track startups to growth stage and make them investment-ready.

Who can apply for Sarthak?

Early-stage startups and businesses registered under DPIIT that generate consistent revenues for at least 6 months and are seeking systematic capital scale-up can apply.

What kind of funding is offered?

Sarthak offers equity-based seed funding ranging from ₹15 lakhs up to ₹30 lakhs, tailored to your growth milestone requirements and valuations.

What support does the program provide apart from funding?

In addition to funding, you will receive 1-1 expert mentorship, post-funding compliance and milestone support, strategic financial modeling, and exclusive investor networking access.

Is there a fee to apply for the program?

No, there is absolutely no application fee or program fee associated with the Sarthak Program.

How long is the program?

The core incubation and investment-readiness curriculum lasts for 12 weeks, followed by ongoing post-investment monitoring and advisory support.

What sectors are eligible?

We are sector-agnostic. We support innovative startups across technology, robotics, agriculture, eco-care, healthcare, consumer products, and more.

Will I need to give equity?

Yes, seed funding is structured as a standard equity-based investment. The specific equity percent is negotiated based on mutual valuation agreement.

How do I apply?

Simply click the "APPLY NOW" button at the top or bottom of this page, fill out the Google Form with your business metrics and pitch deck, and submit.

What is the selection process?

The process involves initial screening of pitch details, followed by an interview round, due diligence, and final presentation to the Sarangi investment committee.

When will funding be disbursed?

Funding is typically disbursed within 4 to 6 weeks post-selection, subject to completing the due diligence process and signing term sheets.

Who are the mentors and investors involved?

You will connect with a curated pool of industry leaders, venture capital advisors, active angel investors, and experienced growth consultants from the Sarangi Consulting network.
