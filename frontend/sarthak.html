About the program

Turn Ideas Into Investable Businesses.

SARTHAK is India's premier Startup Pitch & Investment Platform, designed to help early-stage founders move from ambition to solid execution. We position ambitious founders to become investment-ready by validating core ideas, refining business models, and building a clear pathway to growth.

Many early-stage startups first need market validation, expert mentoring, and strategic alignment before they attract capital. Through a selective process, we provide the ultimate ecosystem access to prepare your venture for experienced mentors and potential investors.

Our Methodology

The Growth Timeline

Ideate

Dive into the Ideation Phase with activities designed to spark innovation and strategic insights. Engage in intensive brainstorming sessions, conduct thorough market research, analyze competitors, and develop initial concepts.

Validate

Leverage the valuable resources provided by top incubators and early-stage accelerators, which include comprehensive mentoring and networking opportunities. Test your products and business models directly in the competitive marketplace.

Accelerate

Access advanced resources and tailored accelerator programs designed to help early-stage startups effectively scale rapidly. Focus on refining your product, building your strong team, and preparing for significant future growth phases.
