Startup Document Readiness

People & Leadership Documents

Strong organizations are built on clear agreements. These foundational documents protect founders, align teams, and create the structure for sustainable leadership.

1
Founder Agreement

Defines roles, responsibilities, equity splits, and expectations between all co-founders — the most important leadership document to get right from day one.

3
Co-founder Exit Clause

Defines what happens if a co-founder exits — vesting schedules, buyback rights, and IP assignment. Succession planning starts here.

6
ESOP Agreement

Employee Stock Option Plan documentation and vesting schedules — a critical leadership tool to attract, retain, and align your best talent around long-term success.

10
Employee Contracts

Formal employment agreements covering job scope, compensation, notice period, IP ownership, and confidentiality — the backbone of organizational design.

11
Offer Letters

Clear, compliant offer letters that set accurate expectations on role, CTC, and joining conditions — ensuring every new hire is aligned before they walk through the door.

12
HR Policies

Leave policies, code of conduct, POSH compliance, and performance frameworks that define a professional, accountable, and growth-oriented workplace culture.
