Essential Startup Documents

Every founder needs the right paperwork in place. We help you build and review the 18 critical documents every startup must have — from incorporation to investment readiness.

Why Document Readiness Matters

Missing or poorly drafted documents expose your startup to legal disputes, investor red flags, and operational chaos. We audit, draft, and organise every document your startup needs — so you can focus on building, not firefighting.

Business Impact
  • Investor-ready from Day 1
  • Legal risk reduced significantly
  • Cleaner co-founder relationships
  • Faster due diligence cycles
  • Compliance from the start
Founder & Equity

Ownership & Co-founder Clarity

Get the foundations right before everything else.

1
Founder Agreement

Defines roles, responsibilities, equity splits, and expectations between all co-founders to prevent disputes.

2
Incorporation Documents

Legal registration of your company — MoA, AoA, Certificate of Incorporation, and related filings.

3
Co-founder Exit Clause

Defines what happens if a co-founder leaves — vesting schedules, buyback rights, and IP assignment on exit.

4
Shareholders' Agreement

Governs rights and obligations of all shareholders, including voting rights, dividend policy, and share transfer restrictions.

5
Cap Table

A live record of who owns what — equity, options, convertibles — essential for every funding round and board decision.

6
ESOP Agreement

Employee Stock Option Plan documentation, vesting schedules, and grant letters to attract and retain top talent.

IP & Confidentiality

Protect What You Build

Your ideas and code are your biggest assets — guard them.

7
NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)

Protects sensitive business information shared with investors, vendors, employees, or potential partners.

8
IP Assignment Agreement

Ensures all intellectual property created by founders and employees is legally owned by the company, not individuals.

9
Trademark / IP Documents

Trademark registration for your brand name and logo, and patents or copyrights for proprietary products or content.

People & HR

Build Your Team Right

Clear documentation keeps your team aligned and legally protected.

10
Employee Contracts

Formal employment agreements covering job scope, compensation, notice period, IP ownership, and confidentiality.

11
Offer Letters

Clear, compliant offer letters that set accurate expectations on role, CTC, and joining conditions before onboarding.

12
HR Policies

Leave policies, code of conduct, POSH policy, and other HR frameworks that define a professional, compliant workplace.

Legal & Compliance

Stay Legally Sound

Non-negotiable documents that protect your business and users.

13
Terms of Service

Sets the rules of engagement between your product and users, limiting liability and defining acceptable use.

14
Privacy Policy

Mandatory disclosure of how you collect, store, and use user data — required for DPDP compliance and app store listings.

15
Legal Compliance Docs

GST registration, PAN, TAN, labour law compliances, and any sector-specific regulatory approvals your startup requires.

Investor Readiness

Win Funding Confidently

Documents investors ask for — have them ready before the ask.

16
Pitch Deck

A crisp, compelling 10–15 slide deck covering problem, solution, market, traction, team, and ask.

17
Financial Model

A 3–5 year projection covering revenue, expenses, unit economics, and funding milestones — your business case in numbers.

18
Term Sheet (if funded)

The foundational deal document outlining valuation, investment amount, board rights, and key investor protections.

Our Methodology

1

2

Draft & Customise

3

Review & Align

We walk founders through every document so you understand what you're signing and why it matters.

4

Maintain & Update
