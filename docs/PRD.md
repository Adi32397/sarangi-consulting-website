# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Sarangi Consulting requires an enterprise-grade digital consulting platform that functions as:

* Consulting Firm Website
* Lead Generation Engine
* CRM System
* Assessment Platform
* Knowledge Center
* Proposal Management System
* Analytics Hub
* AI-Powered Consulting Assistant

The platform must position Sarangi Consulting alongside leading consulting firms while providing operational capabilities that support lead acquisition, client conversion, consulting delivery, and future organizational expansion.

---

# PRODUCT VISION

Create a digital consulting ecosystem that:

Generates qualified business leads

Builds consulting authority

Automates consulting workflows

Provides business intelligence

Uses AI to improve consulting efficiency

Scales into a multi-company Sarangi ecosystem

---

# BUSINESS PROBLEM

Current consulting firms face challenges:

Manual lead handling

Fragmented client information

Poor lead qualification

Limited scalability

Weak knowledge distribution

Time-consuming proposal creation

Lack of analytics

Inefficient client onboarding

The Sarangi platform solves these challenges through automation, intelligence, and centralized operations.
